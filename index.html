Dr. Tukaram Narayan Shinde

I/c. Principal

Anusayabai Ramchandra Burla Mahila Varishtha Mahavidyalaya was established in August, 1990. Currently, it is affiliated to Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holakar Solapur University, Solapur, Maharashtra state. It caters the needs of Telugu Linguistic Minority student. It also strives for linguistically, economically and culturally weaker sections of the society.
